A cross-linked database of highly symmetric combinatorial structures.

Built around *maniplexes* — a unifying framework that captures graphs, maps on surfaces, and abstract polytopes in a common language. Maniplexes are matched via canonical forms to existing resources such as the House of Graphs and Potočnik's [censuses of highly symmetric tetravalent graphs](https://graphsym.net).
